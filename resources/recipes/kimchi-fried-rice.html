Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Kimchi fried rice is a tasty twist on a classic dish.
This is done by adding kimchi and your choice of meat.
Ham is commonly used, but any meat works fine.

Ingredients

Note: this recipe yields 4 servings; Adjust accordingly.

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 drops sesame oil, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 scallions, white and light green parts, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cubed, fully cooked ham
  • 1/3 cup chopped kimchi
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Warm butter and olive oil together over medium-high heat in a large skillet or wok.
  2. Add in sesame oil
  3. Add onion, carrots, and scallions and cook until onion is translucent, about 2 minutes.
  4. Mix in cubed ham and kimchi; cook for 1 more minute.
  5. Season with garlic powder.
  6. Stir in rice and cook until mixture is heated throughout.
  7. Stir until well combined and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
