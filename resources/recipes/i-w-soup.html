Instant Pot® Italian Wedding Soup Recipe

Description

A delicious and hearty soup with meatballs, pasta, chopped vegetables, and cheese.
This recipe uses an Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped carrots
  • 1 cups sliced celery
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 20 Italian meatballs
  • 1/2 cup pastina pasta
  • 6 ounces baby spinach
  • 4 teaspoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Turn on a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant Pot®) and select Saute function. Pour in oil and heat until hot.
  2. Add carrots, celery, and red onion; cook until tender, 4 to 5 minutes.
  3. Season with parsley, basil, and pepper. Pour in chicken broth and add meatballs.
  4. Close and lock the lid. Close the valve and select the Soup function according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 3 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
  5. Release pressure carefully using the quick-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 5 minutes.
  6. Unlock and remove the lid. Add pastina and stir to combine. Replace the lid.
  7. Close the valve and select the Soup function according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 3 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
  8. Release pressure carefully using the quick-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 5 minutes.
  9. Add spinach and stir. Let soup sit for 5 minutes so pasta will be fully cooked and spinach will be wilted.
  10. Season with salt to taste.
  11. Ladle into bowls and top with Parmesan cheese.
